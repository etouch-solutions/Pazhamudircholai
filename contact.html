Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does construction typically take?

Construction timeline varies based on project size and complexity. Typically, a 1500 sq ft house takes 6-8 months, while larger projects may take 10-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide design services?

Yes, we offer complete architectural design services including floor plans, elevations, 3D visualization, and interior design consultation as part of our comprehensive construction packages.

What is included in the quoted price?

Our quotes include all construction work, materials, labor, electrical, plumbing, flooring, painting, and project management. Land cost, government approvals, and furniture are not included.

Do you offer warranty on construction?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive warranty covering structural work (10 years), electrical and plumbing (5 years), and finishing work (2 years). Terms and conditions apply.
