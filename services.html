Our Services

Comprehensive construction solutions for your dream home

Architectural Design

Custom architectural plans and 3D visualization

Our expert architects create detailed plans including floor plans, elevations, sections, and 3D renderings. We ensure optimal space utilization and aesthetic appeal while following all building codes and regulations.

  • Custom floor plans and layouts
  • 3D visualization and walkthroughs
  • Structural design and engineering
  • Building permit assistance
  • Interior design consultation

Electrical Works

Complete electrical installation and safety systems

Professional electrical installation including wiring, lighting, power outlets, circuit breakers, and safety systems. We use ISI marked cables and branded electrical fittings for maximum safety and durability.

  • Complete house wiring with ISI cables
  • LED lighting and fixture installation
  • Power outlets and switch installation
  • MCB panel and safety systems
  • Smart home automation ready

Plumbing

Modern plumbing systems and fixtures

Complete plumbing solutions including water supply, drainage, sanitary fittings, and modern fixtures. We use high-quality CPVC pipes and branded fittings for long-lasting performance.

  • Water supply and drainage systems
  • CPVC pipes and quality fittings
  • Bathroom and kitchen fixtures
  • Water tank installation
  • Sewage treatment systems

Painting & Finishing

Premium paints and professional finishing

Interior and exterior painting with premium quality paints. Includes putty work, primer, and multiple coats for a smooth, durable finish that enhances your home's aesthetic appeal.

  • Interior and exterior painting
  • Wall putty and primer application
  • Texture and decorative finishes
  • Waterproofing solutions
  • Color consultation and design

Flooring & Tiling

Premium flooring solutions and tile work

Professional flooring installation including ceramic tiles, vitrified tiles, marble, and granite. Expert tile laying with proper waterproofing and finishing for all areas including bathrooms and kitchens.

  • Ceramic and vitrified tile installation
  • Marble and granite flooring
  • Bathroom and kitchen tiling
  • Waterproofing and grouting
  • Designer patterns and layouts

Project Management

End-to-end project coordination and supervision

Comprehensive project management including timeline planning, quality control, material procurement, and regular progress updates. Our experienced project managers ensure smooth execution from start to finish.

  • Project planning and scheduling
  • Quality control and supervision
  • Material procurement and logistics
  • Regular progress reporting
  • Budget management and cost control

Civil Construction

Foundation, structure, and masonry work

Complete civil construction services including foundation work, structural construction, masonry, and concrete work. We ensure strong, durable structures built to last generations.

  • Foundation and excavation work

  • Structural repairs and renovation

Doors & Windows

Quality doors, windows, and hardware installation

Installation of doors, windows, and hardware with focus on security, aesthetics, and functionality. We offer various materials including wood, aluminum, and UPVC options.

  • Wooden and UPVC door installation

  • Hardware and accessories
  • Custom designs and sizes

Landscaping
